Newmonic

Mobile App Case Study

By Alejandra Quintanilla

An app designed for students to improve their study habits. Every feature was created to help them achieve their goals, earn the grades they desire and prioritize what is important.

Students with a busy schedule need to manage their time to study because they struggle to stay on task with learning and completing assignments to achieve a good grade.

Newmonic is a mobile app to help students prioritize and manage their time to improve study habits.

User Research

We started off by conducting interviews and survey to understand how students currently set up goals and track their progress. We would like to find any pain points that students are facing currently and identify ways to improve their study habits and their physical and emotional well-being in regards of studying.

  • Interview
    Conduct 5 interviews to find pain points that students are facing and to get in-depth insights
  • Survey
    Create and conduct a quick survey to see average study / work hours and how students manage their time from quantitative data
  • Ideation
    Based on the quantitative and qualitative data from our research, find the root of pain points and devise a solution.

Problem Statement

Based on our findings, students use calendars to organize time blocks for work, school and homework. However during study time, students may drift off from the task at-hand. They may even spend more time studying than expected, especially if they are struggling to learn a specific subject. Other tasks are sidelined and left to finish last minute. Students like Sam with a busy schedule need to manage their time to study because they struggle to stay on task with learning and completing assignments to achieve a good grade.

How might we help Sam prioritize his time to improve his study habits so he can earn good grades in school?
